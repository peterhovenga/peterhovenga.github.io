Interstate subjects meeting Victorian prerequisites

Prerequisite studies listed in CourseSearch are compulsory for course entry. These are listed as studies from the Victorian Certificate of Education (VCE). If you are studying in another Australian state, or studying the International Baccalaureate (IB), you can use the tool below to check which studies from your home curriculum will meet Victorian prerequisites.

Please note:The subjects listed refer to current subjects only. If the name and subject code do not match your results, refer to the Interstate subject guide to Victorian prerequisites on the Reports and Statistics page.
