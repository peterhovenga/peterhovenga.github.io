Estimate your course selection rank

Subject adjustment points are expressed as aggregate points and are applied to the aggregate, not ATAR. Use the adjustment tool below to estimate your course selection rank.

Subject adjustment Tool

To estimate your course selection rank, select your estimated ATAR range then enter the number of adjustment points you think you are eligible for.

Estimate should be used as a guide only.

Some institutions also award adjustment points in recognition of circumstances of disadvantage or rural and regional location that may have affected your education.

It is important to note that adjustment points do not change your actual ATAR and are only relevant during the selection process.
